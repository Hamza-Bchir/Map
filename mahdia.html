Introduction

The Governorate of Mahdia is one of the governorates of Tunisia, situated on the east coast of the country. Known for its historical significance and coastal beauty, Mahdia is a region with a rich cultural heritage and economic importance.

Geography

Mahdia covers an area of approximately 2 878 square kilometers and is characterized by its coastal landscapes along the Mediterranean Sea. The governorate is known for its beautiful beaches and the historical city of Mahdia.

Economy

Mahdia has a diverse economy, with contributions from industries such as fishing, agriculture, and tourism. The governorate's strategic location along the coast has made it a hub for economic activities, including trade and commerce.

Tourism

Mahdia is a popular tourist destination in Tunisia, attracting visitors with its sandy beaches, historical sites, and vibrant culture. The city of Mahdia, with its medina and ancient monuments, is a key highlight for tourists exploring the governorate.

Introduction

The Governorate of Jendouba is one of the governorates of Tunisia, located in the northwestern part of the country. Known for its agricultural importance and scenic landscapes, Jendouba is a region with a rich cultural and historical heritage.

Geography

Jendouba covers an area of approximately 3 100 square kilometers and is characterized by lush greenery and mountainous terrain. The governorate is known for its beautiful landscapes and natural attractions.

Population

The population of the Governorate of Jendouba is around 401 477. Jendouba is home to a diverse community, and its cultural traditions are influenced by a mix of Arab and Berber heritage.

Agriculture

Jendouba is renowned for its agricultural activities, with fertile lands supporting the cultivation of various crops. The governorate plays a crucial role in Tunisia's agricultural sector, contributing to the nation's food production.

Famous People

Salah Mejri is a Tunisian professional basketball player for Kazma. He also represents the senior Tunisian national basketball team internationally. Standing at 7 ft 2 in, he plays at the center position.

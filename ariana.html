Introduction

The Governorate of Ariana is one of the governorates of Tunisia, situated in the north-eastern part of the country. With a rich history and vibrant community, Ariana is known for its cultural heritage and modern development.

Geography

Ariana covers an area of approximately 482 square kilometers and is characterized by a mix of urban and suburban landscapes. The governorate includes various districts and is well-connected to the capital city, Tunis.

Population

The population of the Governorate of Ariana is around 576 088. It is a diverse community with a blend of local residents and immigrants. The governorate is known for its educational institutions and cultural events.

Economy

Ariana has a growing and diverse economy, with contributions from various sectors such as commerce, services, and technology. The governorate's strategic location and modern infrastructure make it an attractive hub for businesses.

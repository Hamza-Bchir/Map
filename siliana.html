Introduction

The Governorate of Siliana is one of the governorates of Tunisia, situated in the northwestern part of the country. Known for its natural beauty and cultural heritage, Siliana is a region with a diverse landscape and historical significance.

Geography

Siliana covers an area of approximately 4 642 square kilometers and is characterized by its green hills, forests, and agricultural land. The governorate is known for its scenic beauty and is a destination for nature enthusiasts.

Economy

Siliana's economy is based on agriculture, with the cultivation of grains, olives, and fruits being prominent. The governorate's fertile land and favorable climate contribute to the success of agricultural activities in the region.

Culture

The culture of Siliana is enriched by its historical sites and traditional crafts. The governorate is known for its festivals, folk music, and vibrant cultural events that celebrate the unique heritage of the region.

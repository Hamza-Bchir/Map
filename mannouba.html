Introduction

The Governorate of Manouba is one of the governorates of Tunisia, located in the northern part of the country. Recognized for its educational institutions and economic activities, Manouba is a region with a mix of urban and rural areas.

Geography

Manouba covers an area of approximately 1 137 square kilometers and is characterized by its diverse landscapes. The governorate includes urban centers like Manouba city and extends to more rural areas, contributing to its unique geographical features.

Education

Manouba is known for its educational institutions, including the University of Manouba. The governorate has played a significant role in the academic and intellectual development of Tunisia, with a focus on research and higher education.

Economy

The economy of Manouba is diverse, with contributions from various sectors such as agriculture, services, and industry. The governorate's strategic location and economic activities have made it an important center for commerce and development.

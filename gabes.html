Introduction

The Governorate of Gabes is one of the governorates of Tunisia, located in the eastern part of the country. Known for its coastal beauty and economic activities, Gabes is a region with a unique blend of tradition and development.

Geography

Gabes covers an area of approximately 7 166 square kilometers and is situated along the Mediterranean coast. The governorate features a mix of coastal plains and inland areas, contributing to its diverse landscape.

Population

The population of the Governorate of Gabes is around 374 300. With a mix of urban and rural communities, Gabes is known for its cultural richness, reflected in its traditions, music, and festivals.

Economy

Gabes has a significant economic role in Tunisia, with contributions from industries such as agriculture, fishing, and petrochemicals. The governorate's strategic location and economic activities make it a key player in the national economy.

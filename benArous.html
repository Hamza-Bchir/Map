Introduction

The Governorate of Ben Arous is one of the governorates of Tunisia, situated in the northern part of the country. With a mix of urban and suburban areas, Ben Arous is known for its economic activities and cultural vibrancy.

Geography

Ben Arous covers an area of approximately 761 square kilometers and is strategically located near the capital city, Tunis. The governorate features various towns and neighborhoods with diverse landscapes.

Population

The population of the Governorate of Ben Arous is around 631 842. With a dynamic and diverse community, Ben Arous is known for its cultural events, festivals, and communal activities.

Economy

Ben Arous has a thriving economy, with contributions from industries, commerce, and services. The governorate's proximity to Tunis and well-developed infrastructure make it a hub for various businesses and economic activities.

Zaouia Sidi Ben Arous

This 16th-century mausoleum was built on the site of a funduq, in which the 13th-century mystic and professor Ahmed Ben Arous once lived.
A small prayer room on the right of the tomb is still used by his followers.

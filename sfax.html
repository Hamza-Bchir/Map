Introduction

The Governorate of Sfax is one of the governorates of Tunisia, located on the east coast of the country. Recognized for its economic importance and industrial activities, Sfax is a region with a vibrant urban center and strategic significance.

Geography

Sfax covers an area of approximately 7 545 square kilometers and is characterized by its coastal location along the Mediterranean Sea. The governorate includes the city of Sfax, which is the second-largest city in Tunisia.

Economy

Sfax is a major economic hub, with contributions from industries such as fishing, agriculture, and manufacturing. The governorate's port is a key factor in facilitating trade, making Sfax an essential player in Tunisia's economic landscape.

Industry

Sfax is known for its industrial activities, including oil refining, petrochemicals, and textile manufacturing. The governorate plays a crucial role in the country's industrial development, contributing to employment and economic growth.

Zaouia

This zaouia contains the remains of the saint Abou Ishak Jebnani, it is a place of pilgrimage and worship that honors the saint.

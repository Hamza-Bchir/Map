Introduction

The Governorate of Beja is one of the governorates of Tunisia, located in the northwest part of the country. Known for its historical significance and natural beauty, Beja is a region with a rich cultural heritage.

Geography

Beja covers an area of approximately 3740 square kilometers and is characterized by a diverse landscape including plains and mountains. The governorate is home to several towns and villages, each with its unique charm.

Population

The population of the Governorate of Beja is around 303 032. The residents of Beja are known for their warmth and hospitality, contributing to the cultural richness of the region.

Culture

Beja is proud of its cultural heritage, with traditions deeply rooted in history. The governorate hosts various cultural events and festivals that showcase the local arts, crafts, and folklore.
